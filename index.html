What is Thanis?

Thanis is a containment framework for complex narrative and symbolic systems. It helps architects and researchers identify instability patterns, enforce boundaries, and sustain coherent behavior under iterative or adversarial pressure.

  • Precise definitions and taxonomy of instability
  • Reference architecture for containment and governance
  • Practical playbooks for implementation and testing
